Here is my current pourover recipe:
Use a 15g dose of lightly roasted coffee that has been rested for 2-6 weeks with 250g water at 90-95 deg C (1:16 ratio). Start with a medium-coarse grind.
00:00 to 00:40 - 50g Bloom. This release the CO2 in the coffee allowing for better extraction. Without this coffee will taste sour.
00:40 to 01:00 - 25g Second Bloom. This will let the rest of the CO2 come out of the bed and prime the coffee for a better extraction.
01:00 to 02:00 - 175g Pour. Try not to create a turbulent flow as it will clog the filter. You can use a spoon to guide the water over the coffee bed.
Aim for a 2-2.5 min brewing time. Adjust the coffee grind accordingly.
My current coffee roaster - Hydrangea Coffee. Equipment I use - Hario Switch with made in Japan Hario filters or Kalita Wave with Kalita filters.
